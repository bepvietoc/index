About Us


Welcome to Bếp Việt OC! We are a small family-run kitchen located in Garden Grove, California, offering authentic Vietnamese holiday staples: Bánh Tét and Bánh Chưng. Our dishes are handmade with love and tradition, using fresh ingredients wrapped in fragrant banana leaves — perfect for sharing or gifting.
